The Importance of Sleep for Brain and Heart Health

Quality sleep supports memory, mood regulation, and cardiovascular health. Poor sleep increases the risk of obesity, diabetes, and heart disease. Adults should aim for 7–9 hours per night.

Good sleep hygiene includes maintaining a consistent bedtime, creating a dark and quiet sleep environment, avoiding screens before bed, and limiting caffeine. Sleep is a crucial pillar of overall health.
